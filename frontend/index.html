Delhi EV Conversion & Retro-Fitment: Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about legal EV conversion in Delhi

Who Can Convert?

  • Petrol, Diesel, and CNG vehicles
  • Auto-rickshaws, Two-wheelers, Goods carriers
  • Must meet age and fitness standards
  • Only government-approved conversion kits
  • Authorized installers only
Important: 10-year-old diesel vehicles and 15-year petrol/CNG vehicles require simultaneous approval and registration.

Important Guidelines

  • DIY conversions are NOT legal
  • Non-approved kits prohibited
  • Unauthorized workshops not allowed
  • Only ARAI/ICAT approved kits
  • Certified installers mandatory
Note: All conversions subject to availability of Type approved kits for your vehicle make and model.

Step-by-Step EV Conversion Process

Complete guide to legal EV conversion and registration

1

Identify Vehicle Eligibility

Check if your vehicle is eligible for retro-fitment:

  • Verify vehicle type and age requirements
  • Ensure availability of Type approved kit for your make/model
  • Find authorized retro-fitment centre
  • For 10-year diesel/15-year petrol vehicles: simultaneous approval required
Resources: Check approved kit manufacturers and authorized dealers on Switch Delhi website
2

Identify Correct Retrofitter

Choose government-approved conversion partners:

  • Find manufacturer/importer of pure electric retro-fitment kit
  • Check authorized retro-fitment dealers list
  • Verify kit compatibility with your vehicle model
  • Ensure dealer is authorized by Transport Department
3

Prior Approval (Form 22C)

Submit retro-fitment request as per CMVR Rule 47A:

Required Details:
  • Vehicle Registration Number
  • Vehicle Make & Model
  • Manufacturing Month & Year
  • Vehicle Category
  • Existing Fuel Type
Kit Information:
  • Kit Manufacturer/Importer Name
  • Kit Name & Identification Number
  • Retro-fitment Centre Details
  • Centre Address
Timeline: Permission granted/rejected within 7 working days. If no response, approval is deemed granted.
4

Vehicle Retrofitting

Get your vehicle converted at approved centre:

  • Approach approved Electric Retro-fitment Centre (ERFC)
  • Use only approved Pure Electric Kit
  • Ensure proper installation by certified technicians
  • Obtain installation certificate
5

Registration & Documentation

Complete the legal process:

Required Documents:
  • Original Registration Certificate (RC)
  • Owner's ID & Address Proof
  • Kit Approval Certificate
  • Installation Certificate
  • Application Form & Form 22 Part-I
  • Scrapping certificate (if applicable)
Final Steps:
  • Vehicle inspection by authorities
  • Safety and compliance checks
  • Updated RC with electric fuel endorsement
  • Apply for subsidies and benefits
  • Receive new registration certificate
